Who are we?

Miyawaki is created by former Michelin Executive Director Ahn Yujin, in 2005 after leaving the company. Her goal is to introduce the world's best restaurants to the people with the combined experience of her renowned Critic Team, and the people's votes. For the first time, customers and clientele are able to decide which restaurants deserve the renown that they get.

Decide on the top restaurants for the people, by the people. You have the power to change the entire food critic industry. Rate and review the restaurants you go to and be ingrained in a close-knitted community of chefs and critics.

Join the Miyawaki Chefs and chat with like-minded members. Get recommendations for restaurants to look out for, and form relationships that share a common interest in food.

Learn more about the food on your plate and expand your knowledge.

Never be confused on where to eat anymore. With Miyawaki, you will be ensured 100% enjoyment in your next meal and enjoy exclusive benefits like prioritised reservations and a chance to meet the masterminds behind the world's finest dishes.
